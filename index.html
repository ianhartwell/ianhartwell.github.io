About Me

Welcome to my portfolio site. This site is being put together during my time working through the Codecademy Full-Stack Developer course. It is a way to showcase the skills that I am learning along the way. So far I have studied HTML, CSS and some basic JavaScript.

I began this course while being off work with a shoulder injury and unable to perform my usual day job due to the manual nature and constant heavy lifting that it required. I hope that by taking and completing this course that I will be able to transition into a new career.

I already have some database skills that I have acquired having completed the Goolge Data Analytics Professional certificate on Coursera, link available here, and I hope to be able to apply this knowledge later in the course when it combines both front-end and back-end web development.
